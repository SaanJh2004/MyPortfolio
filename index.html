ABOUT

VERSATILE VOCALIST & GUITARIST

I am a singer and musician rooted in Indian music. I work across Bollywood, Sufi, and contemporary fusion styles. My singing focuses on expression, melody, and emotion. Rhythm comes naturally to me, and I rely on feel more than force.

I like mixing traditional influences with modern arrangements. The goal is to keep the sound familiar but not dated. Something that feels honest and current.

Along with singing, I play electric guitar. I use it to shape the mood of a performance. Sometimes through simple riffs. Sometimes through ambient layers or lead parts that push the energy forward.

I am drawn to music that tells stories and connects quickly with listeners. Whether it is a small acoustic setting or a full band show, I focus on creating performances that stay with people even after the music stops.
